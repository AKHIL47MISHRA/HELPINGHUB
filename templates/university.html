About the University

The foundation of Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University (originally Agra University) was laid on the 1st of July, 1927, through efforts of Rev. Canon A.W. Davis, Munshi Narain Prasad Asthana, Dr L.P. Mathur, Lala Dewan Chand, Rai Bahadur Anand Swaroop, and Dr Brajendra Swaroop.

Original jurisdiction extended over United Provinces of Agra, Central India and Rajputana with 14 affiliated colleges and 2530 students, of which 1475 belonged to United Provinces.
