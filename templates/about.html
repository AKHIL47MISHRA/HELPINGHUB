Project Description

HelpingHub is an AI-based self-growth web application designed to help students and individuals improve their academic performance, coding skills, mental & physical health, productivity, and daily problem-solving.

The platform uses multiple AI models through OpenRouter API to provide real-time, personalized assistance in different domains — education, health guidance, coding, self-analysis, and utility tools.

Each AI service is powered by a specialized model with tailored system instructions: Gemini 2.0 Flash for academic tasks, Mimo V2 for coding, DeepSeek R1 for health, and GPT-OSS-120B for general growth and dream analysis.
